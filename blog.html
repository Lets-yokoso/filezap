What is every format?

🌐
WEBP
Google's modern image format. Smaller than JPG, supports transparency like PNG.
Image 📕
PDF
Portable Document Format. Fixed layout, works everywhere. But editing it? Good luck.
Doc ✏️
SVG
Scalable Vector Graphics. Infinite resolution, tiny file size. Perfect for logos & icons.
Image 🎬
MP4
The king of video. H.264 codec, works on every device. It's the safe choice.
Video 📝
DOCX
Microsoft Word's format. XML under the hood. Editable, rich formatting, widely supported.
Doc 🎞️
GIF
The OG animated image. 256 colors only. Huge file size. But we love it anyway.
Image 🎵
MP3
Lossy audio compression. Small files, good enough quality. The format that killed CDs.
Audio 📱
HEIC
Apple's iPhone format. Half the size of JPG at same quality. But zero Windows support.
Image
